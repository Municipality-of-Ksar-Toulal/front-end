About Us

We are a team of passionate professionals

The municipality of Ksar Toulal is an official urban community in the Meknes Province of the Fez-Meknes region. It was established in 1992 by two official decrees. The municipality covers an area of approximately 2,723 hectares and has a population of approximately 13,000 to 14,000, with a large proportion of active youth. The municipality has a fully integrated administrative apparatus, including administrative and technical staff, with seven new employees recently hired. The municipality is managed by a communal council of 15 councilors. The municipality is responsible for providing basic services to residents, such as cemetery management, infrastructure, sanitation, security, and the improvement of public facilities. Despite some challenges, such as the cemetery being full and the need for infrastructure development, the municipality of Ksar Toulal is a dynamic urban area that seeks to improve its services by modernizing its administration and strengthening communication with the local community.
